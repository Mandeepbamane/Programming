About us

Established in 2004, Institute of Business Studies & Research (IBSAR) is a premier management school.

The institute was set up in Navi Mumbai (the fastest growing industrial hub), to give an impetus to management education, and ever since has been acting as the pioneer in the field of management education interfacing with industry, business leaders and academicians.

IBSAR is a multi disciplinary business school focusing on actionable business programs. We ensure that our education is at all times relevant to business.

We deliver well trained business leaders and managers who are able to influence across organizational boundaries.

We are fully committed to high quality research. We continuously invest in research activities and recruitment of academic leaders in fields such as Strategy , OB , Finance , Marketing , Retail , Operations , General Management and IT .
